Alcoutim Acupuncture and Massage

We offer a simplified and holistic approach to healing. Our treatments foster a healthy and vibrant internal state for our clients and help them to achieve a powerful emotional and physiological mind and body.


Treatments

Massage

Deep stimulation of the tissues that surround the skeleton and internal organs fan help increase the flow of blood and energy around the body.

Acupuncture

Acupuncture is part of the ancient practice of Traditional Chinese medicine where needles are used to stimulate the energy pathways of the body to clear energy blockages and promote mental and physical well being.

Gua Sha

Gua sha is a natural, alternative therapy that involves a massage tool applied to the tissues to improve circulation promote health and treat chronic pain.
